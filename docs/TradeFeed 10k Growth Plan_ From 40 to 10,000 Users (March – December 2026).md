# TradeFeed 10k Growth Plan: From 40 to 10,000 Users (March – December 2026)

This plan is designed for a **solo developer** using **Cursor AI and Copilot** to execute at high speed. It focuses on high-impact, low-effort tactics tailored for the South African market.

---

## 1. Current State Analysis

### The Product Right Now
TradeFeed is a "WhatsApp-first" e-commerce enabler. It solves the primary pain point for South African micro-sellers: **the chaos of manual WhatsApp DMs.**
- **What's Working:** The AI-powered "Photo to Listing" feature is a massive hook. The marketplace provides immediate value, and the "WhatsApp Checkout" aligns with how 90% of local informal trade happens.
- **What's Missing:** Essential trust pages (FAQ, How-it-works) are currently 404. The marketplace has low density (avg 2.5 products/seller), suggesting many users drop off after listing 1-2 items.
- **Conversion Bottlenecks:** The jump from Free (20 products) to Starter (R99) is purely based on volume. For a small reseller, 20 products might be enough, meaning they never have a reason to pay.

## 3. Feature Roadmap (Prioritized by Impact)

### Next 30 Days: The "Trust & Friction" Phase
- **Fix 404s:** Restore FAQ and How-it-works. Use AI to generate 20+ common SA seller questions.
- **WhatsApp "Magic Link" Login:** Remove passwords. Let users login via a WhatsApp OTP or link.
- **Bulk Image Upload:** Let sellers dump 50 photos at once; AI processes them in the background.
- **Why:** Reduces drop-off during onboarding. *Dev Time: 3-5 days with Cursor.*

### Next 60 Days: The "Conversion" Phase
- **Inventory Alerts:** WhatsApp notification when stock is low (requires simple stock tracking).
- **Buyer Reviews:** Allow buyers to leave a rating after a WhatsApp order is "Delivered."
- **Why:** Reviews build trust for the marketplace; inventory alerts make the tool "sticky." *Dev Time: 7 days.*

### Next 90 Days: The "Professional" Phase
- **Custom Domains:** Let Pro users link `myshop.co.za`.
- **Printable QR Codes:** Generate a "Scan to Shop" PDF for physical stalls/taxis.
- **Why:** High perceived value for Pro tier. *Dev Time: 5 days.*

### Q3 & Q4: The "Scale" Phase
- **Automated Ad Generator:** One-click "Create Facebook/Instagram Ad" for a product.
- **Multi-Staff Accounts:** For larger wholesalers in Jeppe/Fordsburg.
- **Why:** Drives user growth (ads) and justifies the R299/R499 tiers.

## 4. Growth Channels: Solo Dev Strategy

### Rank 1: WhatsApp/Facebook "Supplier" Groups (High Impact, Low Cost)
- **Tactic:** Join "Jeppe Wholesale," "Dragon City Suppliers," and "SA Resellers" groups.
- **Action:** Don't spam. Offer a "Free AI Catalog Audit." Help one big supplier list their 100 products. Their 500+ resellers will see the link and want their own.

### Rank 2: TikTok "Side Hustle" Content (High Impact, Low Cost)
- **Tactic:** Record 60-second "How I built a shop in 2 minutes" videos.
- **Action:** Use the "Photo to Listing" feature as the "wow" factor. Tag #SAMA28 #SideHustleSA.

### Rank 3: Taxi Rank / Physical Hubs (Medium Impact, Medium Effort)
- **Tactic:** Print 1,000 stickers with a QR code: "Stop sending 100 photos. Get a WhatsApp Catalog."
- **Action:** Stick them near Jeppe Street, Bree Taxi Rank, and MTNs.

### Rank 4: Paid Ads (Start in July)
- **Budget:** Start with R100/day on Facebook/Instagram.
- **Targeting:** "WhatsApp Business Users," "Facebook Marketplace Sellers," "Small Business Owners South Africa."

## 5. Conversion & Retention

### How to Convert Free to Paid
1. **Feature Gating:** Keep "Unlimited Products" for Starter. Move "Buyer Reviews" and "Inventory Alerts" to Starter.
2. **The "First Sale" Trigger:** Send a WhatsApp congrats when they get their first order. Offer a 50% discount on Starter for the first 3 months.
3. **AI Credits:** 10 is too many for a test, too few for a pro. Keep it at 10, but offer "Refill packs" or Unlimited in Pro.

### Retention Strategy
- **Weekly "Sales Report":** Send a WhatsApp every Monday: "Your catalog had 450 views and 12 orders last week. Top product: [X]."
- **Community:** Create a "TradeFeed Insiders" WhatsApp group for the first 100 sellers. Listen to their feature requests.

---

## 6. Solo Developer Productivity

- **Cursor AI / Copilot:** Use "Composer" in Cursor for multi-file features. Don't write boilerplate; describe the logic and let AI generate the CRUD.
- **Automate Support:** Use a WhatsApp Bot (via Tiledesk or similar) for basic "How do I list?" queries.
- **What NOT to Build:** Do NOT build a custom payment gateway. Stick with PayFast/Yoco redirects. Do NOT build a native app.
