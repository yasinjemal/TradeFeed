### The Problem TradeFeed Solves

South African wholesalers and small retailers **already use WhatsApp** to discuss prices and place orders ‚Äî but they do it **manually** with:
- Text messages
- Voice notes
- Screenshots of price lists
- Phone calls

This manual process leads to:
- ‚ùå Lost orders
- ‚ùå No order history or tracking
- ‚ùå No inventory visibility
- ‚ùå No analytics or reporting
- ‚ùå Time wasted on repetitive communication

**TradeFeed digitizes what they're already doing** ‚Äî turning WhatsApp-shared catalogs into a professional, trackable ordering system.
